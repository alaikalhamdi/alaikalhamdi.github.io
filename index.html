About Me

Hello! I'm a developer with a passion for creating sleek, functional, and user-friendly websites and software. I have experience with various web technologies and always strive to improve my skills and learn new ones.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Indonesia, where I have been actively involved in various projects and organizations. My journey in tech started with a curiosity for how things work, which led me to explore programming and web development.

In addition to my technical skills, I have a strong interest in design and user experience. I believe that a great product is not only functional but also visually appealing and easy to use. This belief drives me to continuously improve my design skills and stay updated with the latest trends in UI/UX.

I also enjoy participating in hackathons, attending tech meetups, and contributing to open-source projects. Outside of tech, I also enjoy robotics and games! I would also come to comic conventions as an otaku!

Feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to collaborate on a project or just have a chat about technology and innovation!
